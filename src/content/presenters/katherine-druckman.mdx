# {frontmatter.presenterName}
Katherine Druckman is an Open Source Evangelist at Intel Corporation. She is a software engineer and content creator with over a decade of experience in engineering, content strategy, product management, user experience, and technology evangelism. A long-time Drupal enthusiast, and former Director of Digital Experience for Linux Journal.She is the co-host of the podcast Reality 2.0 and also frequently co-hosts the FLOSS Weekly & Tech Break shows on the Twit Tv network.
