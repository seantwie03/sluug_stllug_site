# {frontmatter.presenterName}
Joe Brockmeier is a open
source evangelist for Citrix and committer on
the Apache CloudStack project. Joe has also worked for Novell as
the
openSUSE community manager, and has a long history of involvement
with
open source. He has also spent many years working as a
technology
journalist, and has written for ReadWriteWeb, Linux.com, LWN,
Linux
Magazine, NetworkWorld, ZDNet, and many others.
