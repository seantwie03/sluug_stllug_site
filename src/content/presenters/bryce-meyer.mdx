# {frontmatter.presenterName}
Bryce Meyer is a Senior
Member of Technical Staff at The Software Engineering Institute at
Carnegie Mellon University. He is specializing in Acquisition
Improvement with an emphasis on Army programs. Prior to joining the
SEI, he was the Managing Partner of Maptes Advanced Projects, LLC,
an Internet networking consulting company, where he managed
consultants to provide network architecture, operations process,
network security assessment, and network systems integration and
design. ...previously an active duty U.S. Air Force officer and
Assistant Professor at the Virginia Military Institute.
