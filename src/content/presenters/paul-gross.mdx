# {frontmatter.presenterName}
Paul Gross is currently
a Ph. D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science and
Engineering at Washington University in St. Louis. His research
focuses on enabling non-programmers to independently learn computer
programming by reusing code from existing examples. As part of his
research, Paul is a developer of the Looking Glass programming
environment which is targeted at motivating middle school students
to learn computer programming by enabling them to create
interactive 3D stories and games.
